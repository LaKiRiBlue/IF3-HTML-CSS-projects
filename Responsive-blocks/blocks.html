IS THERE ANYTHING CUTER THAN A GERBIL?

Gerbil fact file

There are many species of gerbil, though the Mongolian gerbil is the one most commonly kept as a pet. Gerbils are burrowing rodents and are naturally adapted to desert environments in Africa, India and Asia. They keep themselves busy by digging, and they also like to gnaw. Gerbils can live for up to 3-4 years - some even live longer. Gerbils shred material for nesting, which also helps to keep their teeth short (their teeth never stop growing!).
